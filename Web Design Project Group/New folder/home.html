About Us


At Café Blue, nestled in the heart of Dundrum, Ireland, we’re more than just a coffee company—we’re a family. Founded with a passion for quality and community, we source our beans from sustainable farms around the globe, ensuring every cup supports ethical practices. Our cozy café is a warm gathering spot where friends and families come together to enjoy handcrafted brews and homemade treats. With a commitment to excellence and a love for our craft, we invite you to experience the rich flavors and friendly atmosphere that define Café Blue. Join us in celebrating the simple joys of life, one delicious cup at a time!
